Pathfinding Algorithms

A classic problem for intelligent agents is to find the shortest path from point A to point B. As a fun short side project I spent a bit of time experimenting with two algorithms: breadth and depth first searching methods. Additionally I used depth first searching methods to generate the maze itself.
